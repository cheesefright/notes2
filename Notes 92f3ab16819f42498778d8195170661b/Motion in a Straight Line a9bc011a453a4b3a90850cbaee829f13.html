Rectilinear Motion : Motion in a straight line

Distance : Total Length from A to B

Displacement : Shortest possible length from A to B

∴Displacement≤Distance\therefore \text{Displacement} \le \text{Distance}∴Displacement≤Distance﻿

Uniform Motion

if an object moving along a straight line covers equal distance in equal interval of time.

  • Average Speed = DistanceTime\frac{\text{Distance}}{\text{Time}}TimeDistance​﻿ (Scalar)
  • Average Velocity = DisplacementTime\frac{\text{Displacement}}{\text{Time}}TimeDisplacement​﻿(Vector)
